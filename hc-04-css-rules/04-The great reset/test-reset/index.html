Tapu Lele Raids

Tapu Lele is a Tier 5 legendary raid boss in Pokémon Go and marks its debut in the Lush Jungle Event 2022, running from March 22nd, 10 AM – March 29th, 8 PM, 2022.

Tapu Lele is currently a case of lost potential as it doesn’t learn Charm in Pokémon Go (which it can learn in the Main Series Games). Even from the Psychic POV, its moves aren’t the best. So while Tapu Lele doesn’t dazzle in either of its typing, it can experience a change of fortunes with something like Charm being added to its moveset in the future. Anyway, it is still a legendary Dex addition, to say the least.

Pokemon counter of tapu lele raids:

  • Mega Gengar
  • Giratina Origin
  • Chandelure
